ABOUT-US

A Learning Place for Amiable Muslimahs for the Memorization of the Holy Qur'an and Arabic Texts. It was established
in the Year 2021 to cater to the need of the Muslim children in the society.

OUR AIM

Our aim is to groom individuals who practice prestine Islam and relate with people with good manners

OFFERED SUBJECT

Hifzul Qur'an, Arrabiyah (Dursuul lugah, Nahwu, Sarf), Tawheed, Fiqh Nisaa, Al-Hadith, Adaabu Taalibul'ilm, Al-Hisaab.

ADDITIONAL COURSES

We offer Coding and Hairdressing helping the children with an Additional skill.
